Explore Rwanda 🇷🇼

Discover misty mountains, gorilla trekking forests, stunning lakes, rolling green hills, and one of Africa’s cleanest and most scenic countries.

🏙️ Cities & Culture

Kigali

Clean and modern capital city surrounded by green hills.

Huye

University town with cultural heritage and museums.

Musanze

Gateway to volcanoes and gorilla trekking region.

Rubavu

Lakeside town on Lake Kivu with beach vibes.

🌿 Nature & Volcanoes

Volcanoes National Park

Famous for mountain gorillas and misty volcanoes.

Nyungwe Forest

Ancient rainforest with chimpanzees and canopy walks.

Akagera National Park

Savanna park with lions, elephants, and lakes.

Lake Kivu

One of Africa’s Great Lakes with calm beaches.

🧭 Adventure & Wildlife

Gorilla Trekking

Rare close encounter with mountain gorillas.

Kigali Memorial

Important historical site and remembrance center.

Mount Bisoke

Volcanic hike with crater lake at the summit.

Lake Muhazi

Peaceful lake ideal for boating and relaxation.

🏔️ Scenic Hills & Views

Land of a Thousand Hills

Rolling green landscapes across Rwanda.

Kibuye

Scenic lakeside town with peaceful views.

Gisenyi

Beach town on Lake Kivu with mountain backdrop.

Tea Plantations

Green hills covered in scenic tea farms.
