About Lake Nakuru Park

World-famous for thousands of flamingos, rhino sanctuary, and scenic viewpoints over the alkaline lake.

Top Attractions

Witness pink carpets of flamingos along the lake.

Rhino Sanctuary

Protected area for endangered white and black rhinos.

Baboon Cliff Viewpoint

Stunning panoramic views of the entire park.

Travel Tips

Best birdwatching in early morning. Carry binoculars and camera with zoom lens.
