About Nairobi

Kenya’s capital where urban life meets wildlife. Nairobi is the only city in the world with a national park within its borders, blending skyscrapers with safari landscapes.

Top Attractions

Nairobi National Park

Wild lions and giraffes with city skyline views.

David Sheldrick Elephant Orphanage

Rescue center for orphaned baby elephants.

Giraffe Centre

Feed and interact with endangered Rothschild giraffes.

Travel Tips

Best visited in dry seasons (June–October). Combine city tour with a half-day safari experience.
