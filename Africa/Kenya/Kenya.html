Explore Kenya 🇰🇪

Discover the Great Rift Valley, safari adventures, Mount Kenya, Maasai culture, savannas, and world-famous wildlife reserves.

🏙️ Cities & Culture

Nairobi

Capital city with wildlife parks inside urban landscapes.

Mombasa

Coastal city with beaches, forts, and Swahili culture.

Kisumu

Lakeside city on the shores of Lake Victoria.

Nakuru

Gateway to national parks and flamingo-filled lakes.

🦁 Safari & Wildlife

Maasai Mara

World-famous safari park and Great Migration location.

Amboseli

Elephants with Mount Kilimanjaro backdrop.

Tsavo National Park

One of Africa’s largest wildlife reserves.

Lake Nakuru

Famous for flamingos and rhino sanctuary.

🏔️ Nature & Landscapes

Mount Kenya

Second highest mountain in Africa with alpine views.

Great Rift Valley

Massive geological trench with lakes and cliffs.

Lake Victoria

Africa’s largest lake with fishing villages.

Hell’s Gate

Unique park with cliffs, gorges, and cycling safaris.

🏖️ Beaches & Coast

Diani Beach

White sand beach with turquoise Indian Ocean waters.

Watamu

Marine parks, coral reefs, and snorkeling paradise.

Lamu Island

Ancient Swahili town with traditional architecture.

Malindi

Historic coastal town with coral reefs and beaches.
