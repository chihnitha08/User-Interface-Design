About Watamu

Watamu is a peaceful coastal town known for its marine national park, coral gardens, mangroves, and crystal-clear waters perfect for snorkeling.

Top Attractions

Watamu Marine Park

Protected coral reefs filled with colorful fish and sea turtles.

Mangrove Forests

Kayak through serene mangrove channels along the coast.

Snorkeling Spots

Clear shallow waters ideal for beginner snorkelers.

Travel Tips

Visit during low tide for the best snorkeling visibility and calm waters.
