About Diani Beach

Diani Beach is Kenya’s most famous coastal paradise with powdery white sand, coral reefs, and crystal-clear Indian Ocean waters.

Top Attractions

Coral Reefs

Snorkel and dive among vibrant marine ecosystems.

Colobus Conservation

A sanctuary protecting the rare Angolan colobus monkeys.

Beach Walks

Relax with scenic walks along the palm-lined shore.

Travel Tips

Visit during dry seasons (Dec–Mar, Jul–Oct) for the best beach and water visibility.
