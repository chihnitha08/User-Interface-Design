About Nakuru

A gateway to stunning Rift Valley scenery, famous for flamingos, rhinos, and panoramic viewpoints over Lake Nakuru.

Top Attractions

Lake Nakuru National Park

Home to flamingos, rhinos, and diverse birdlife.

Baboon Cliff

Best panoramic viewpoint over the lake and park.

Menengai Crater

Massive volcanic crater with hiking trails.

Travel Tips

Carry binoculars for bird watching. Early morning game drives offer best wildlife sightings.
