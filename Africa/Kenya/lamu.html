About Lamu

A UNESCO World Heritage Swahili town with narrow streets, dhow boats, coral stone houses, and rich coastal culture.

Top Attractions

Lamu Fort

Historic fort overlooking the seafront.

Dhow Sailing

Traditional sailing experience at sunset.

Shela Beach

Quiet sandy beach perfect for relaxation.

Travel Tips

No cars on the island—explore on foot or by donkey. Visit during Lamu Cultural Festival.
