About Mombasa

A historic Swahili coastal city known for white sand beaches, coral reefs, and centuries-old forts shaped by Arab and Portuguese influence.

Top Attractions

Fort Jesus

16th-century Portuguese fort overlooking the Indian Ocean.

Old Town

Narrow streets with Swahili architecture and markets.

Nyali Beach

Relaxing beach with turquoise waters and palm trees.

Travel Tips

Ideal for beach lovers. Visit Fort Jesus in the evening for cooler weather and sunset views.
