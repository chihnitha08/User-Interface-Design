About Kisumu

A peaceful lakeside city on Lake Victoria known for fishing culture, sunsets, and wetlands rich in birdlife.

Top Attractions

Lake Victoria Shore

Beautiful lakeside views and fishing villages.

Kisumu Impala Sanctuary

Small wildlife sanctuary with impalas and birds.

Dunga Hill Camp

Popular spot for sunset views and local cuisine.

Travel Tips

Evenings offer the best lake views. Try fresh tilapia from local lakeside restaurants.
