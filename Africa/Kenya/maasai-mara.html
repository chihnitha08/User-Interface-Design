About Maasai Mara

Kenya’s most famous safari destination, known for the Great Migration and incredible sightings of the Big Five across endless savannah plains.

Top Attractions

The Great Migration

Millions of wildebeest and zebras crossing the Mara River.

Big Five Safari

Spot lions, elephants, leopards, rhinos, and buffalo.

Maasai Village Visit

Experience the traditional culture of the Maasai people.

Travel Tips

Visit between July and October for the migration. Early morning and evening safaris are best.
