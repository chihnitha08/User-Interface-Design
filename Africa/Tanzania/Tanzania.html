Explore Tanzania 🇹🇿

Discover the Serengeti safari plains, Mount Kilimanjaro, Zanzibar beaches, Ngorongoro crater, and breathtaking African wilderness.

🏙️ Cities & Culture

Dar es Salaam

Largest city and coastal gateway to Tanzania.

Arusha

Safari capital at the foot of Mount Meru.

Mwanza

Lakeside city on the shores of Lake Victoria.

Dodoma

Capital city surrounded by hills and savanna landscapes.

🦁 Safari & Wildlife

Serengeti

World-famous safari park and Great Migration site.

Ngorongoro Crater

Massive volcanic caldera full of wildlife.

Tarangire

Elephant-rich park with giant baobab trees.

Nyerere (Selous)

One of Africa’s largest protected wilderness areas.

🏖️ Beaches & Islands

Zanzibar

Exotic island with white sand beaches and Stone Town.

Pemba Island

Quiet tropical island with coral reefs.

Nungwi Beach

Turquoise waters and sunset dhow sailing.

Kendwa Beach

Relaxed beach with soft sand and nightlife.

🏔️ Mountains & Nature

Mount Kilimanjaro

Africa’s highest peak and iconic snow-capped mountain.

Lake Manyara

Flamingos, forests, and tree-climbing lions.

Mount Meru

Dramatic volcanic mountain near Arusha.

Lake Tanganyika

One of the deepest and oldest lakes in the world.
