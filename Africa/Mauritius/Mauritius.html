Explore Mauritius 🇲🇺

Discover turquoise lagoons, coral reefs, luxury beaches, volcanic landscapes, waterfalls, and tropical island culture in the Indian Ocean paradise.

🏙️ Cities & Culture

Port Louis

Capital city with markets, waterfronts, and colonial history.

Curepipe

Highland town known for cool climate and shopping streets.

Grand Baie

Tourist hotspot with nightlife, beaches, and resorts.

Mahébourg

Historic coastal town with museums and calm lagoons.

🏖️ Beaches & Lagoons

Flic en Flac

Long white sand beach with sunset views and coral reefs.

Le Morne

Iconic mountain beach and UNESCO heritage site.

Trou aux Biches

Calm lagoon perfect for snorkeling and swimming.

Blue Bay

Marine park with crystal clear water and coral reefs.

🌿 Nature & Waterfalls

Seven Coloured Earth

Natural sand dunes with unique rainbow colors.

Chamarel Waterfall

Highest waterfall in Mauritius surrounded by jungle.

Black River Gorges

National park with hiking trails and wildlife.

Alexandra Falls

Scenic viewpoint waterfall in dense forest.

🧭 Adventure & Marine

Île aux Cerfs

Island paradise with water sports and beaches.

Casela Nature Park

Safari park with lions, ziplining, and adventure rides.

Tamarin Bay

Surfing and dolphin watching hotspot.

Grand Bassin

Sacred volcanic lake and Hindu pilgrimage site.
