Explore Namibia 🇳🇦

Discover towering dunes, endless deserts, wildlife safaris, coastal shipwrecks, and some of Africa’s most surreal landscapes.

🏙️ Cities & Towns

Windhoek

Capital city blending African and German colonial heritage.

Swakopmund

Coastal desert town with German architecture and adventure sports.

Walvis Bay

Harbor city famous for flamingos and dunes meeting the ocean.

Lüderitz

Atlantic coastal town with colonial buildings and shipwrecks.

🏜️ Desert Landscapes

Sossusvlei

Iconic red sand dunes among the tallest in the world.

Dead Vlei

Ancient white clay pan with dead camel thorn trees.

Namib Desert

One of the oldest deserts on Earth with vast dunes.

Spitzkoppe

Granite mountain formations rising from desert plains.

🦁 Wildlife & Safari

Etosha National Park

Premier safari park with elephants, lions, and salt pans.

Caprivi Strip

Lush river region with hippos and crocodiles.

Waterberg Plateau

Cliff-top sanctuary for rare wildlife species.

Khaudum Park

Remote wilderness with untouched safari experience.

🏖️ Coast & Shipwrecks

Skeleton Coast

Foggy coastline with whale bones and shipwrecks.

Cape Cross

Seal colony with thousands of Cape fur seals.

Sandwich Harbour

Dunes dropping directly into the Atlantic Ocean.

Kolmanskop

Abandoned diamond mining ghost town in the desert.
