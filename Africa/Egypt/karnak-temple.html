About Karnak Temple

Karnak Temple is one of the largest religious complexes ever built, dedicated primarily to the god Amun. It showcases massive columns, obelisks, and ancient carvings from multiple Pharaoh dynasties.

Top Attractions

Hypostyle Hall

Forest of giant sandstone columns covered in hieroglyphs.

Obelisks of Hatshepsut

Towering stone monuments dedicated to the gods.

Sacred Lake

Ancient ceremonial lake within the temple complex.

Travel Tips

Visit with a guide to understand the rich history. Late afternoon offers softer light for photography.
