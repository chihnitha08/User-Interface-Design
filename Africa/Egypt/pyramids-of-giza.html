About Pyramids of Giza

The Pyramids of Giza are one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, built over 4,500 years ago as royal tombs for Egyptian Pharaohs.

Top Attractions

Great Pyramid of Khufu

Largest and oldest pyramid in Giza.

Great Sphinx

Mythical guardian statue of the pyramids.

Desert Panorama

Golden Sahara views around the pyramids.

Travel Tips
