About Valley of the Kings

This royal burial ground near Luxor holds the tombs of Egypt’s greatest Pharaohs, including Tutankhamun. The valley is famous for richly decorated underground tombs carved into desert rock.

Top Attractions

Tomb of Tutankhamun

The most famous tomb discovered with treasures intact.

Tomb of Ramses VI

Incredible ceiling artwork and colorful hieroglyphics.

Desert Cliffs

Stunning desert landscape surrounding the royal valley.

Travel Tips

Photography may be restricted inside tombs. Go early morning to avoid heat and crowds.
