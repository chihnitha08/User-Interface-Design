Explore Morocco 🇲🇦

Discover ancient medinas, Sahara deserts, blue cities, Atlas Mountains, coastal towns, and rich Arab-Berber culture.

🏙️ Imperial Cities

Marrakech

Vibrant city with souks, palaces, and Jemaa el-Fnaa square.

Fes

Ancient city with one of the world’s oldest medinas.

Casablanca

Modern coastal hub and economic capital of Morocco.

Rabat

Capital city blending history, ocean views, and modern life.

🏜️ Sahara & Desert

Merzouga

Gateway to the Sahara dunes and camel desert treks.

Zagora

Desert town famous for palm valleys and Sahara routes.

Erg Chebbi

Golden sand dunes stretching into the horizon.

Draa Valley

Oasis valley with palm groves and ancient kasbahs.

🏖️ Coastal Cities

Essaouira

Windy coastal town with beaches and historic forts.

Agadir

Modern beach resort city on the Atlantic coast.

Asilah

White-walled artistic coastal town.

Tangier

Gateway between Africa and Europe with rich history.

🏔️ Mountains & Nature

Atlas Mountains

Snow-capped peaks and Berber villages.

Ourika Valley

Lush green valley with waterfalls near Marrakech.

Chefchaouen

Famous blue city in the Rif Mountains.

Akchour

Waterfalls and natural rock bridges.
