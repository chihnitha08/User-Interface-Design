Explore Zimbabwe 🇿🇼

Discover powerful waterfalls, ancient ruins, safari parks, massive lakes, and breathtaking African landscapes.

🏙️ Cities & Culture

Harare

Capital city with modern life, markets, and gardens.

Bulawayo

Cultural hub with colonial history and museums.

Mutare

Gateway to eastern highlands and scenic mountains.

Masvingo

Historic town near Great Zimbabwe ruins.

🦁 Safari & Wildlife

Hwange National Park

Largest park in Zimbabwe with elephants and lions.

Mana Pools

UNESCO park along the Zambezi River.

Zambezi National Park

Wildlife near Victoria Falls river basin.

Matobo Hills

Rock formations with rhinos and ancient art.

🌊 Natural Wonders

Victoria Falls

One of the world’s largest and most powerful waterfalls.

Lake Kariba

Huge man-made lake with fishing and sunsets.

Eastern Highlands

Mountains, forests, and misty valleys.

Chinhoyi Caves

Blue limestone caves with deep crystal pools.

🏛️ Heritage & History

Great Zimbabwe Ruins

Ancient stone city and UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Khami Ruins

Historic stone ruins near Bulawayo.

National Gallery

Art and cultural exhibitions of Zimbabwe.

Railway Museum

Historic trains and colonial transport history.
