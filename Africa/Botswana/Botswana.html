Explore Botswana 🇧🇼

Discover vast savannas, the Okavango Delta, wildlife safaris, salt pans, deserts, and some of Africa’s most untouched natural wonders.

🏙️ Cities & Culture

Gaborone

Capital city with modern culture and local markets.

Francistown

Historic mining city and gateway to northern Botswana.

Maun

Safari capital and entry point to the Okavango Delta.

Kasane

River town near Chobe National Park and wildlife zones.

🦁 Safari & Wildlife

Chobe National Park

Home to elephants, lions, and river safaris.

Moremi Game Reserve

Rich biodiversity inside the Okavango Delta.

Okavango Delta

World-famous inland delta and wildlife paradise.

Savuti

Predator-rich region known for lion and hyena encounters.

🏜️ Desert & Salt Pans

Kalahari Desert

Endless sand dunes and unique desert wildlife.

Makgadikgadi Pans

Vast salt flats that glow under the sun and stars.

Nxai Pan

Seasonal wildlife migration and baobab trees.

Tsodilo Hills

Ancient rock art site and spiritual heritage landmark.

🧭 Adventure & Nature

Tuli Block

Remote wilderness with elephants and rugged landscapes.

Khama Rhino Sanctuary

Conservation area protecting endangered rhinos.

Boteti River

Wildlife corridor with seasonal animal migrations.

Central Kalahari

One of the largest game reserves in the world.
