About

Varirata National Park is a rainforest park near Port Moresby known for birdwatching, hiking trails, and scenic viewpoints.

Top Attractions

Bird Watching

Rare birds of paradise sightings.

Forest Trails

Walking paths through rainforest.

Viewpoints

Panoramic views of Port Moresby.

Wildlife

Kangaroos, birds, and native species.

Travel Tips

Best visited early morning for wildlife spotting.
