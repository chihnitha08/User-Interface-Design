Explore Papua New Guinea 🇵🇬

Discover untouched jungles, tribal cultures, volcanic islands, WWII history, and some of the world’s most remote natural wonders.

🏙️ Cities & Culture

Port Moresby

Capital city with museums, coastline views, and cultural centers.

Goroka

Highland town famous for coffee and cultural festivals.

Mount Hagen

Mountain city surrounded by lush valleys and tribes.

Lae

Industrial hub with coastal access and botanical gardens.

🌿 Nature & Highlands

Mount Wilhelm

Highest peak in PNG with alpine trekking routes.

Sepik River

Long river system with tribal villages and carvings.

Karawari River

Remote jungle river with rich wildlife and culture.

Varirata National Park

Rainforest park near Port Moresby with birdwatching views.

🏝️ Islands & Beaches

Milne Bay

Crystal clear waters and diving paradise.

Madang

Coastal town with lagoons, reefs, and tropical islands.

New Britain

Volcanic island with rainforests and beaches.

Tufi Fjords

Rare tropical fjords with coral reefs.

🧭 Adventure & History

Kokoda Track

Historic WWII trekking route through mountains and jungle.

Rabaul

Volcanic town with war history and active volcano views.

Bismarck Sea

Diving and marine biodiversity hotspot.

Tari Valley

Famous for Huli wigmen tribes and mountain scenery.
