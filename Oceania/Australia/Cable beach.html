About Cable Beach

Cable Beach in Broome is famous for its 22 km stretch of white sand, turquoise waters, camel rides, and breathtaking sunsets over the Indian Ocean.

Top Attractions

Camel Sunset Ride

Ride along the beach on camels while watching the golden sunset over the ocean.

White Sand Shore

Relax on soft white sand with crystal-clear turquoise ocean views.

Sunset Viewpoint

One of Australia’s best sunset spots with magical orange skies.

Water Adventures

Enjoy swimming, kayaking, and relaxing in calm ocean waters.

Travel Tips

Visit during sunset for the best experience. Carry sunscreen, water, and enjoy camel rides in the evening.

Cable Beach Tourism

A perfect mix of desert, ocean, and sunset magic in Western Australia.
