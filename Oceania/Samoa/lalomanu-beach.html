About Lalomanu Beach

One of Samoa’s most beautiful beaches with crystal clear water, white sand, and palm-lined shores.

Top Attractions

Beachfront View

Relax with stunning turquoise ocean views.

Coral Reefs

Snorkel among colorful marine life.

Beach Huts

Traditional seaside fale huts for relaxation.

Sunrise Point

Perfect spot for early morning views.

Travel Tips

Bring reef-safe sunscreen and visit during calm tide hours.
