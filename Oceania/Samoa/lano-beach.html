About

Lano Beach is a peaceful coastal escape on Savai’i Island, known for its calm waters, white sand shoreline, and traditional Samoan seaside lifestyle. It is perfect for relaxation, swimming, and experiencing local village culture.

Top Attractions

Calm Lagoon

Clear shallow waters ideal for swimming.

Palm Shoreline

Beautiful coconut palm lined beach.

Village Coast

Experience authentic Samoan coastal living.

Sunset Point

Golden sunsets over the Pacific Ocean.

Travel Tips

Bring snorkeling gear, respect village areas, and visit during low tide for the clearest water views.
