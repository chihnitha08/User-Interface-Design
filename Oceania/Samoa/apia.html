About Apia

Apia is the capital city of Samoa, located on the island of Upolu. It blends traditional Polynesian culture with modern island life.

Top Attractions

Maketi Fou Market

Local market full of fresh produce and crafts.

Mulinu’u Peninsula

Historic site with government buildings and ocean views.

Apia Waterfront

Beautiful coastal walkway and harbor scenery.

Cathedral of Apia

Iconic religious landmark in the city center.

Travel Tips

Visit early morning for markets and avoid midday heat.
