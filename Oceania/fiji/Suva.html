About Suva

Suva is the capital of Fiji, known for government buildings, cultural museums, harbor views, and busy markets.

Top Attractions

Fiji Museum

Historical artifacts showcasing Fiji’s rich heritage.

Suva Market

Colorful local market full of fresh produce and crafts.

Albert Park

Open green space for sports and city events.

Travel Tips

Best explored on foot, carry cash for markets, and visit museums early.
