About Nadi

Nadi is one of the most important cities in Fiji, known for its international airport, beautiful temples, vibrant markets, and as the main gateway to Fiji’s islands and resorts.

Top Attractions

Garden of the Sleeping Giant

Famous orchid garden with lush rainforest trails and peaceful scenery.

Sabeto Hot Springs

Natural mud pools and hot springs perfect for relaxation and wellness.

Sri Siva Subramaniya Temple

Colorful Hindu temple and one of the most iconic cultural landmarks in Fiji.

Travel Tips

Visit early morning for markets, carry light clothing, and use Nadi as a base to explore nearby islands.
