About Nausori Highlands

Nausori Highlands is a scenic mountainous region in Fiji known for rolling green hills, traditional villages, and breathtaking panoramic views of the countryside.

Highlights

Hilltop Views

Panoramic views of Fiji’s lush green valleys and mountains.

Village Life

Experience traditional Fijian rural lifestyle and culture.

Scenic Drives

Beautiful winding roads through hills and farmlands.

Tips

Best visited during clear weather for visibility. Carry light jackets as it can be cooler in the highlands.
