About Explore

Explore is an interactive travel exploration platform designed to help users discover dream destinations based on mood, interests, travel style, and companions. Our mission is to make travel planning visually immersive, fun, and personalized.

Who We Are

We are a creative student team passionate about combining technology and travel experiences into one interactive platform. Explore allows users to visually discover destinations and plan trips in an engaging way.

Users can search destinations by mood such as adventurous, relaxing, cultural, foodie, or romantic while also filtering by travel companions and preferences.

Our project focuses on UI/UX design, animations, responsiveness, interactivity, and modern web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
