About Seoul

Seoul is the vibrant capital of South Korea filled with futuristic skyscrapers, K-pop culture, palaces, cafés, shopping streets, and incredible nightlife.

Top Attractions

Han River

Enjoy peaceful night walks, river picnics, and amazing skyline views.

Bukchon Hanok Village

Explore traditional Korean houses and aesthetic historical streets.

Myeongdong

Famous for Korean street food, shopping, and trendy cafés.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: Spring and Autumn.

Use the Seoul subway for easy travel.

Try Korean BBQ, ramen, and street food.

Visit at night for beautiful city lights.
