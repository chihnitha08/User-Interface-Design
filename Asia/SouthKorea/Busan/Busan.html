About Busan

Busan is South Korea’s coastal paradise known for beaches, seafood, colorful villages, and relaxing ocean views.

Top Attractions

Haeundae Beach

One of Korea’s most famous beaches with nightlife, cafés, and beautiful sunsets.

Gamcheon Culture Village

Colorful houses, artistic streets, and amazing photo spots.

Jagalchi Market

Taste fresh seafood and experience Busan’s famous fish market culture.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: Summer and Spring.

Try seafood dishes and Korean street food.

Visit beaches during sunset for stunning views.
