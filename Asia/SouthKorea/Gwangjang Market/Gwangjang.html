About Gwangjang Market

Gwangjang Market is one of Korea’s oldest and most famous street food markets packed with authentic Korean dishes and vibrant local culture.

Crispy traditional pancakes filled with vegetables and seafood.

Dumplings

Taste delicious handmade Korean dumplings and spicy street snacks.

Market Culture

Experience authentic Korean market life filled with energy and flavor.
