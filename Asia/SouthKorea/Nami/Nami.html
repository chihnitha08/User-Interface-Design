About Nami Island

Nami Island is one of South Korea’s most romantic destinations, famous for tree-lined roads, autumn scenery, and peaceful nature.

Tree Roads

Walk through beautiful tree tunnels filled with dreamy seasonal colors.

Nature Walks

Peaceful walking paths surrounded by forests, rivers, and calm landscapes.

Autumn Scenery

Experience magical orange and red leaves during the famous Korean autumn season.
