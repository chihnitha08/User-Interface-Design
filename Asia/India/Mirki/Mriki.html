About Mirik

Mirik is a beautiful hill station in West Bengal, India, known for its peaceful atmosphere, lush tea gardens, and scenic lake views. Located in the Darjeeling district, Mirik is famous for Sumendu Lake, orange orchards, and breathtaking Himalayan landscapes. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers and travelers seeking peace and relaxation.

Top Tourist Places

Sumendu Lake

A peaceful lake surrounded by gardens, pine trees, and boating spots.

Tea Gardens

Enjoy the scenic beauty of lush green tea plantations around Mirik.

Bokar Monastery Viewpoint

A peaceful place offering spiritual calm and stunning mountain views.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: March to June and September to December.
Enjoy boating, horse riding, and local tea tasting for the best experience.
