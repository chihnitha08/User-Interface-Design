About Lucknow

Lucknow is famous for Awadhi cuisine, royal kebabs, and rich Mughlai dishes. The city offers one of India's best food experiences.

Must Try Foods

Galouti Kebabs

Soft melt-in-mouth kebabs famous across India.

Awadhi Biryani

Fragrant biryani cooked with royal spices and flavors.

Sheermal

Traditional saffron flavored bread served with kebabs.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: October to March.
Visit old Lucknow markets for authentic street food.
