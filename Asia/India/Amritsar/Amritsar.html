About Amritsar

Amritsar is famous for Punjabi cuisine filled with butter, spices, and warmth. It is also home to the Golden Temple.

Famous Foods

Amritsari Kulcha

Stuffed crispy kulchas served with spicy chole and butter.

Lassi

Refreshing thick Punjabi lassi topped with fresh cream.

Punjabi Thali

Enjoy authentic Punjabi meals loaded with flavors.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: November to March.
Visit local dhabas for authentic Punjabi food.
