About Meghalaya

Meghalaya, known as the "Abode of Clouds", is famous for green mountains, waterfalls, caves, and living root bridges. It is one of India's best destinations for trekking, nature exploration, and adventure tourism.

Adventure Highlights

Living Root Bridges

Trek through dense forests to discover Meghalaya’s world-famous natural root bridges.

Waterfall Trekking

Explore beautiful waterfalls and enjoy adventurous trekking routes across the hills.

Cave Exploration

Discover mysterious limestone caves and underground adventure spots.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: October to April.

Carry raincoats because Meghalaya receives heavy rainfall.

Wear comfortable trekking shoes for long walks and hikes.

Visit Cherrapunji, Dawki River, and Shillong for unforgettable views.
