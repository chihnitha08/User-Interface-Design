About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, it is a symbol of love and one of the most beautiful monuments in the world. Made of white marble, the Taj Mahal attracts millions of visitors every year.

Main Highlights

Main Mausoleum

The central white marble structure and the heart of the monument.

Mughal Gardens

Beautiful symmetrical gardens surrounding the Taj Mahal.

Yamuna River View

Enjoy the peaceful reflection of the Taj Mahal near the river.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: October to March.
Visit early morning for the best sunrise view and fewer crowds.
