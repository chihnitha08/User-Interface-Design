About Hyderabad

Hyderabad is known as the City of Pearls and is one of India's top food destinations. The city is famous for its rich Mughlai cuisine, spicy biryanis, and flavorful street food.

Must Try Foods

Hyderabadi Biryani

World famous aromatic rice dish loaded with spices and meat.

Haleem

A rich and delicious slow-cooked dish loved during Ramadan.

Irani Chai

Enjoy authentic chai with Osmania biscuits in old city cafes.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: October to February.
Visit Charminar and old city food streets during evenings.
