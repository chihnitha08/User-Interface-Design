About Rishikesh

Rishikesh, located on the banks of the Ganga River, is India's adventure capital. Famous for river rafting, bungee jumping, camping, and yoga retreats, Rishikesh attracts adventure seekers from all around the world.

Adventure Highlights

River Rafting

Experience thrilling white water rafting adventures in the Ganga river.

Bungee Jumping

Jump from one of India’s highest bungee platforms for an unforgettable thrill.

Riverside Camping

Relax beside the Ganga river with bonfires, music, and adventure camps.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: September to April.

Carry light jackets for cool evenings near the river.

Book rafting and bungee activities in advance during peak season.

Visit Lakshman Jhula and Triveni Ghat for the complete Rishikesh experience.
